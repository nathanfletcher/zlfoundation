About Us

Zoomlion Foundation (ZLF) is the Corporate Social Responsibility wing of the Jospong Group of Companies.

Since its inception in 1995, the Jospong/Zoomlion Group of Companies have given donations to many individuals and institutions to support a wide range of initiatives. To formalize its corporate social responsibility role for the Jospong/Zoomlion Group of Companies, the Zoomlion Foundation was legally registered as an independent entity with its own Board of Directors in July 2014.

VISION

Our vision is for all people in Ghana to attain excellent environmental sanitation with improved socio-economic transformation.

MISSION

Our mission is to inspire the youth to champion good environmental and sanitary practices and also invest in communities.

CORE VALUES
Our foundation's core values:
  • Good stewardship - we believe in homegrown solutions as well as taking responsibility for the sustainable development of communities we work in.
  • Caring - we care a lot about the communities we work in.
  • Response - we respond to the emergency needs of our people.
  • Environmentally-friendly - we care a lot about the environment.
  • Meeting needs - we strategically respond to the needs of communities where it matters most.
Partnership Approach

Zoomlion Foundation has noted that a collaborative multi-stakeholder approach is vital to maintaining and replicating the success of initiatives such as Zoomkids Club, which is aimed at inculcating good sanitary habits into children. To achieve behavioural change with regard to environmental sanitation in Ghana, neither Zoomlion Foundation, the government nor civil societies alone can make it happen.

The Foundation plans to scale up the number of clubs from the current 400 to 1000 and would like to partner with civil society organizations, donor agencies and government institutions to work together to create a critical mass of young Ghanaians who inspire attitudinal and behavioural change within the society.

How you can help Zoomlion Foundation

You can support the work of Zoomlion Foundation in several ways. Contact us if you are interested in any of the following:

  • Donate to support the activities of the Zoomkids Club, which will go a long way to inculcate good sanitary habits into children and the youth.
  • Volunteer to establish a Zoomkids Club in a school or community, or support existing clubs with your knowledge on environmental sanitation.
  • Make a donation to any of the thematic area of the Foundation.
  • Make a donation/pledge towards the Endowment fund.

Primary Area Of Focus

The foundation's first focal area is to provide support for the activities of the Zoomkids Club. The Zoomkids Club is the flagship project of Zoomlion Foundation. It is made up of environmental sanitation clubs operating in schools across the country. The aim of the Zoomkids Project is to facilitate the creation of social and environmental awareness and education among young people, as well as help them develop good sanitary habits. The Foundation believes that if children are conscientized early on the benefits of good sanitary practices and habits, those habits will become second nature to them and put them in positions where they could influence social behavioural changes in their families, schools and the community at large.


Other focal areas of the foundation

Each year based on our financial situation, Zoomlion Foundation would prioritize any of these three thematic areas:

  1. Environmental Sanitation Management
  2. Social Support
  3. Livelihood Support
1) ENVIRONMENTAL SANITATION MANAGEMENT

This includes:

Support for water, sanitation and hygiene

The Foundation will support basic infrastructure development related to water, sanitation and hygiene (W.A.S.H) activities/projects using simple innovative technologies. These projects will be done especially in communities where Zoomkids Club activities are vibrant.

2) SOCIAL SUPPORT
The specific areas for support are as follows:
  • Education
  • Under-resourced public institutions
  • Emergency response in disaster situations.
  1. Educational Support

    Zoomlion Foundation will provide grants towards payment of school fees for brilliant but needy students in senior high schools and tertiary institutions. Additionally, school teachers who double as Zoomkids Club Patrons may obtain scholarship assistance to pursue a postgraduate degree course at our African Institute of Sanitation and Waste Management (AISWAM-KNUST).

  2. Support to Under-resourced institutions

    Zoomlion will identify, partner and collaborate with institutions, non-governmental organizations and corporate entities to support under-resourced institutions like hospitals, prisons, schools for the disabled, orphanages, etc. The Foundation will also support some specific church activities.

  3. Emergency response

    In an unfortunate event of an epidemic outbreak such as cholera or a natural disaster in the contry, based on its financial situation, Zoomlion Foundation will join forces with institutions that share a common goal to provide the needed support.

3) LIVELIHOOD SUPPORT
Support for recycling initiatives

The Foundation will support and/or partner with grassroots organizations that are transforming their economic lives through recycling initiatives.

Apply for support

Who can apply for assistance?
  • Grassroots organizations/self-help groups/community-based organizations
  • Under-resourced public institutions (e.g. community clinics, prisons, etc.)
  • Schools where Zoomkids Clubs operate
  • Zoomkids Club Patrons
  • Individual needy but brilliant students who have qualified for public senior high school or tertiary institutions
  • Religious-based groups

How to apply

Zoomlion Foundation accepts requests throughout the year. Interested individuals or organizations should send an e-mail or letter of not more than a thousand words stating the main purpose of the request. Zoomlion Foundation will send application guidelines to the applicant if the request meets its criteria for support. Applicants must note that even though Zoomlion Foundation would have wished to provide support for all, due to limited resources not every request would be honored.
