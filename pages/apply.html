Apply for support

Who can apply for assistance?
  • Grassroots organizations/self-help groups/community-based organizations
  • Under-resourced public institutions (e.g. community clinics, prisons, etc.)
  • Schools where Zoomkids Clubs operate
  • Zoomkids Club Patrons
  • Individual needy but brilliant students who have qualified for public senior high school or tertiary institutions
  • Religious-based groups
Size of support

Zoomlion Foundation will vet and select projects submitted for approval by a 5-member board who will meet every quarter to consider requests. The project categories are as follows:

Micro Below GHC 10, 0000.00
Small GHC 11, 000.00 - 49,000.00
Medium GHC 50,000.00 - 200,000.00
Large Above GHC 200,000.00
How to apply

Zoomlion Foundation accepts requests throughout the year. Interested individuals or organizations should send an e-mail or letter of not more than a thousand words stating the main purpose of the request. Zoomlion Foundation will send application guidelines to the applicant if the request meets its criteria for support. Applicants must note that even though Zoomlion Foundation would have wished to provide support for all, due to limited resources not every request would be honored.

Alternatively, you could also apply via the form below if you're using a desktop or a laptop computer
